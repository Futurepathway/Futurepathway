Smart Study Tips

1. Use the Pomodoro Technique

Study for 25 minutes, then take a 5-minute break. Repeat 4 times, then take a longer break. This improves focus and prevents burnout.

2. Active Recall

Instead of re-reading notes, close your book and try to recall key points. It boosts long-term memory.

3. Spaced Repetition

Review information at increasing intervals: after 1 day, 3 days, 7 days, etc. Apps like Anki can help automate this.

4. Mind Mapping

Create visual maps of topics to understand and connect concepts better. Great for subjects like Biology and History.
